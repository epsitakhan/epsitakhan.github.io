Flow

Date Sun 11 December 2022



A sort of conciousness and awareness
Not borne of inattention or stupor
Rather something accquired from routine
The harness of control still present
No longer chafing, but gliding
Smoothly against well worn-out grooves
Soothing and quietly exhilirating
Like the feathers of a bird
Grazing the surface of water
Ever so lightly as it soars
Like the briefest, just-right tension
On a string of a cello
In the midst of an unremarkable trello.
