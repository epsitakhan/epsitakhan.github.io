Internal Damnation

Date Mon 12 December 2022

The pang creeps up unexpectedly
Not unlike a metacognitive scalpel
With its twisted, unforgiving sterility
Its nature? Fickle but insidious
Its methods? Without a doubt, pernicious
As the sutures become undone, one by one
And old wounds lay open, gaping wide
I am compelled to do what must be done
Confront that from which I so desperately hide
The part of me that slowly withered away
Dead, now only to serve a perfunctory reminder
Some punitive life-remote stuck on replay
The part that looked outward with hope and wonder
That dared to imagine another way to cope
Under the weight of all those failed dreams, ventures
Like walking on a precarious, raggedy tightrope
Of my very own design — fraught with vices
And when it snaps, and rest assured - it will
That prodding blade, that accosting voice, returns
Anewed with a sense of petulance and vitriolic thrill
It bellows - 'Fool! What will it take for you to learn?
Why entertain such asinine, unrealistic fantasies
Why resist calling a spade a spade
When so evident your glaring incapibilities
Everything part and parcel of your pathetic charade
Even those moments of ephemeral reprieve
Borne of stowing all the misdeeds in the shelf
All cowardly, selfish attempts to deceive
Not just everyone else but yourself'
Deemed sufficient, the castigation halts
One can only wade through this murky morass so much
without wishing for the forbidden release that exalts
Only one remaining task to end this drudge
Fraying twine — retrace the incision and sew
For it's never tied up in a neat little bow
